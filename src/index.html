About Us

Owltree is a vegan apparel brand, created by 4 vegan friends, which focus on creating and delivering high quality and limited edition goods for those who don't want to harm anyone but still want to look stylish.

Everything at Owltree has proudly been
designed, customised, hand printed and finished in Barcelona.

100% vegan goods. Harming no one in the process.
