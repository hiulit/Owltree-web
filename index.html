About Us

OWLTREE is a vegan apparel brand. 4 vegan friends from barcelona how focus on creating and delivering high quality and limited edition goods for those how don't want to harm anyone but still look stylish.

Born from the love for the animals and the planet, OWLTREE aspires to be a little more than just a vegan brand. We are dedicated to collaborate and help animal sanctuaries around the country.

Everything in OWLTREE has proudly been locally sourced, customised, hand printed, tailored and finished in Catalonia. 100% vegan goods. Harming no one on the process.
