About Us

Owltree is a vegan apparel brand based in Barcelona.

Created by 4 vegan friends who focus on creating and delivering high quality and limited edition goods for those how don't want to harm anyone but still look stylish.

Everything in Owltree has proudly been
designed, customised, hand printed and finished in Barcelona.

Born from the love for the animals and the planet, Owltree aspires to be a little more than just a vegan brand. We are dedicated to collaborate and help animal sanctuaries around the country.

100% vegan goods. Harming no one in the process.
